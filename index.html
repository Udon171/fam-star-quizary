HOW TO PLAY FAM STAR QUIZARY

  • 1. Setup
    Start by clicking start to play as 1 player. Then, be given a fun profile icon for each person to represent them in the game. This is your first step to becoming a trivia star!
  • 2. Game Settings
    Next, decide on your challenge level. You can set the difficulty, which determines how much time you have to answer questions, and choose the number of rounds for the game.
  • 3. Countdown
    Before each question, a 3-second countdown will appear over the game interface. Get ready—when the countdown ends, the question will be revealed!
  • 4. The Quiz
    Pay close attention as the questions & there answers. When you think you know the answer, be the fastest one to hit the correct answer.
  • 5. Answering
    After seeing your answer, you’ll have a few seconds to pick the correct answer from the choices on screen. Correct answers earn points—and you get a bonus for being fast! Answers will shake when hovered, and turn green or red when clicked depending on correctness.
  • 6. Victory!
    The winner of the finale is crowned the Fam Star Quizary Champion! Plus, if you win a game on 'Hard' mode, you’ll unlock the super-challenging 'Sick Mode' for an even tougher test of your trivia skills.
