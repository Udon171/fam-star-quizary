How to Play

  • Choose your quiz settings and start a new game.
  • Read each question and select the correct answer before time runs out.
  • Earn points for each correct answer. Wrong answers will shake and show the correct one.
  • Try to get the highest score possible!
  • When the game ends, see your results and play again.
